Format:
-------

1. Introduction:

What is the purpose of this research project?
Summarize the purpose

2. Motivation:

Why is this topic being explored? 
What is your motivation?
Interest?
Importance?

3. Experiments/Use Cases:

Showcase examples of the topic

4. Discussion

- pros/cons
- plus/delta


5. Summary

6. Citations and References


Deliverables:

- Research Report (as above)

- PowerPoint Presentation
